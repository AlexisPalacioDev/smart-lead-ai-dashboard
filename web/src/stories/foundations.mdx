# Smart Lead AI Dashboard Foundations

## Principles

- Dark editorial control-room aesthetic from Stitch
- One primary action per surface
- Loading, empty, error, and success states are explicit
- Data-heavy components prefer clarity over decoration

## Tokens

- Background: `#101418`
- Surface Lowest: `#0a0f13`
- Surface Low: `#181c21`
- Surface: `#1c2025`
- Surface High: `#262a2f`
- Surface Highest: `#31353a`
- Primary: `#00ff41`
- Danger: `#ffb4ab`

## Storybook Coverage Rules

- Every reusable component in `src/components/**` needs a colocated story
- Form, modal, and data states must show happy path plus failure or empty variants
- Stories use deterministic mock data only
